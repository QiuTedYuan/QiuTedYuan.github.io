Yuan Qiu


HELLO!

I am a research fellow cosupervised by Prof. Xiaokui XIAO from NUS and Prof. Bogdan CAUTIS from CNRS@CREATE. I received my Ph.D. degree from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (HKUST), under the supervision of Prof. Ke YI. Prior to that, I received my B.Sc. degree in Mathematics and Economics, and in Computer Science with First Class Honors from HKUST.

My research interest lies in data privacy; database theory and systems; and data sketching.
